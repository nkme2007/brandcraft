# Social Media Formats — Quick Reference

Condensed spec reference for all 9 platforms supported by the Brand Creative Skill. Full specifications including design principles are in `SKILL.md` Section 9.

---

## Global Rules (All Platforms)

- Design at stated px; export at 2x for retina
- Keep all critical content within the **safe zone** — content outside may crop
- Text coverage: **below 20%** of image area (Instagram + Facebook suppress reach above this)
- Minimum font size: **24px body · 36px headlines** at stated dimensions
- Visual hierarchy: one dominant element · one supporting element · one CTA

## Instagram

| Format | Dimensions | Ratio | Safe Zone |
|---|---|---|---|
| Feed square | 1080 × 1080 | 1:1 | 50px all sides |
| Feed portrait ⭐ | 1080 × 1350 | 4:5 | 50px all sides |
| Feed landscape | 1080 × 566 | 1.91:1 | 60px all sides |
| Stories | 1080 × 1920 | 9:16 | Top/bottom 268px · Sides 76px |
| Reels | 1080 × 1920 | 9:16 | Top 14% · Bottom 20% |
| Carousel slide | 1080 × 1080 | 1:1 | 50px all sides |

⭐ = best performing format

## Cross-Platform Quick Pick

| Goal | Best platform+format |
|---|---|
| Maximum feed real estate | Instagram 4:5 portrait |
| Professional B2B reach | LinkedIn 1.91:1 image |
| Viral potential | TikTok 9:16 native video |

| Direct messaging | WhatsApp 9:16 status |
| Local business | Google Business 4:3 post |
